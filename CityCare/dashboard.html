How CityCare Works

For Citizens

  1. Capture a clear photo of the civic issue.
  2. Select the category and provide location details.
  3. Submit your report and track its progress in the History tab.

For Authorities

  1. Review incoming reports in the Management tab.
  2. Update status to "In Progress" when a team is dispatched.
  3. Mark as "Resolved" when the issue is fixed.

Note: For severe emergencies, please dial the local emergency services number directly. CityCare is meant for non-emergency civic maintenance requests.
